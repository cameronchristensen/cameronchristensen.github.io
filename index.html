About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio website. My name is Cameron Christensen, I was born in Riverton, Utah and and have lived in Utah my entire life. Currently, I am a government employee looking for a career change into the web development world. Some of my aspirations as a web developer would be to create massive and marvelous websites for clients and companies to enjoy. I love to travel and enjoy seeing parts of the world where history has played a major role. For example, some of the places that I have visited are Germany, Denmark Czech Republic, Italy, and Greece. Each of the places has a rich history and are filled with amazing stories.

On another trip, I went to Africa to visit a family friend and while doing so, I was able to do some service as well. It was an amazing experience. Many people there don't have running water, but yet they are so happy with what they do have, it's incredible. Sometimes I wonder if we should try to live like them so we don't take the modern conveniences we have for granted.
